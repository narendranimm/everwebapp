We are EVER
Our Story
In the bustling streets of modern cities, where time is a precious commodity and sustainability a growing concern, a visionary concept was born – EVER an electric bike rentals company.

EVER was founded in 2023 by Mr. Raj Kiran Yadav, a critical thinker and problem solver who holds a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering. He started his career in 2013. He has the experience of over 10 years in Various industries like Mahindra Automotives, Hathway and Cyient.

With a commitment to creating greener and more accessible cities, Raj Kiran established EVER . His vision was to offer an efficient, eco-friendly, and affordable transportation alternative that would benefit both the environment and local economies. His mission has been to empower communities through innovation and sustainable practices. Under Raj Kiran's dynamic leadership, EVER has flourished as a pioneer in the electric bike rental sector. He has cultivated a culture of innovation, encouraging his team to explore new frontiers in technology, operations, and customer experience. Raj Kiran has fostered partnerships with local businesses and engaged with riders to ensure that EVER contributes to the growth and well-being of the communities it serves.
